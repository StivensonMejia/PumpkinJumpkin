In this endless forest, your mission is simple but challenging: go as far as possible without hitting any obstacles! Prepare to face a variety of dangers including treacherous spikes, spinning saws, fire-breathing pumpkins, fiery projectiles, evil flying eyes, and obstacles that merge with the environment itself.

Each race is a new adventure, full of adrenaline and excitement. Can you surpass your own limits and reach a record score? Show your skill and reaction speed in this challenging survival game!

And that's just the beginning: with your support, I'm planning to expand this world in future updates. Coming soon, you will be able to enjoy an adventure mode with more structured levels, face challenging bosses and discover an even deeper mystery behind the story of our brave protagonist. Get ready for an exciting experience full of action and surprises in our ever-evolving game!
